Our Story

A farm that
breathes

Pollamgattu is a 15-acre working farm in Chanvelli village, Chevella — 45 km from Hyderabad. We grow vegetables, keep a small orchard, and welcome guests who want to slow down, get their hands dirty, and remember what quiet actually sounds like.

No itineraries. No alarm clocks. Just open fields, wood-fire cooking, and the rhythm of the land.
